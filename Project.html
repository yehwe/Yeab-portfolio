My Projects

Rocket

As a member of the Astronomy Club, I contributed to a rocket project where we designed and built a model rocket capable of reaching impressive altitudes. The project involved hands-on teamwork, from selecting materials to assembling key components like the propulsion system and guidance mechanisms.

Modern Chicken Coop

I have designed a modern chicken coop featuring an automatic ventilation system that maintains optimal airflow for the chickens. It includes a magnetic sensor door that provides security by alerting against intruders. Additionally, a conveyor belt cleaning mechanism ensures efficient waste removal with minimal human involvement.

Stove

I am currently working on a rechargeable stove that harnesses light energy and converts it into stored power for later use. This innovative design aims to provide a sustainable and energy-efficient solution for cooking, especially in areas with limited access to electricity.
